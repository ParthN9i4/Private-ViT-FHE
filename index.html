About Me

Hello, I'm Parth!

I'm a doctoral candidate passionate about bridging Federated Learning (FL) and Homomorphic Encryption (HE) to build privacy-preserving machine-learning systems that handle class imbalance in real-world data.

Currently, I'm developing FLICKER — an end-to-end framework that combines Flower, CKKS encryption, and novel imbalance-aware aggregation to push the boundaries of secure federated learning.

I enjoy translating theory into robust, open-source implementations and sharing my findings with the community.
